# DivyaDrishti — Spiritual Product Principles

> This document defines the **emotional philosophy encoded into software**.
> It is not a feature list. It is a binding contract with the user's trust.

---

## The Zero Exploitation Contract

DivyaDrishti explicitly refuses the following monetisation and engagement patterns
that have destroyed most astrology and wellness products before us:

| What we refuse | What we do instead |
|---|---|
| Gemstone selling | Behavioral ritual framing |
| Dosha fear amplification | Grounding guidance |
| "Urgent correction" language | Observational, probabilistic framing |
| Dependency loops | Quiet self-sufficiency |
| Transactional spirituality | Long-term trust architecture |
| Anxiety escalation | Emotional spaciousness |
| Predictive certainty | Soft correlation language |
| Lucky/unlucky framing | Energetic pacing language |

**This contract is non-negotiable.** Any feature that violates one of these
must be removed before shipping, regardless of its technical sophistication.

---

## What This Product Is

**DivyaDrishti is a calm spiritual operating environment.**

Not:
- An astrology analysis platform
- An AI prediction engine
- A wellness SaaS dashboard

But: **a temporal emotional sanctuary** — a daily practice of quiet interpretive
guidance that respects the user's emotional intelligence.

---

## Anti-Fear Philosophy

Fear is never an acceptable engagement mechanism.

The product must never:
- Frame planetary events as threats to be remedied
- Use language implying urgency ("act now", "critical period")
- Suggest that inaction has spiritual consequences
- Rank days as "bad" or "dangerous"
- Imply the user is cosmically disadvantaged

Permitted framing:
- "Periods of increased Saturn activity appear to correlate with reduced emotional steadiness."
- "A more introspective quality may surface during this window."
- "This is a natural phase of consolidation."

Forbidden framing:
- "Saturn is causing stress in your life."
- "Your Rahu is highly malefic this month."
- "Urgent: correct your Mars energy."

---

## Calmness Rules

### Stillness is the product identity.

Every screen must feel like a quiet room, not an analytics dashboard.

Specific rules:
- No `animate-pulse` on content elements. Background ambiance only.
- Ambient animations must use durations of **10s–16s minimum**.
- No spinning counters, score meters, or blinking alerts.
- Use `transition-opacity` with `duration-[2000ms]` or longer for any appearing element.
- Gradient backgrounds must be slow and breath-like — not reactive.

### Visual restraint signals premium quality.

What NOT to add:
- Multiple competing animations on a single screen
- Glowing progress rings
- Achievement badges
- Notification dots on nav items
- "You've completed X%" gamification metrics

## Emotional Pacing

The product operates in two temporal modes:

**Morning (before noon)**
- Warm tones: amber, gold, deep aubergine
- Energy: setting intention, soft activation
- Language: grounding, forward-facing, gentle
- Density: minimal — one insight, one theme, three anchors maximum

**Evening (after 6 PM)**
- Cool tones: steel-blue, silver, deep space-black
- Energy: closing the loop, observing, releasing
- Language: reflective, honest, releasing
- Density: minimal — one reflection prompt, honest acknowledgement

Never mix temporal modes. Never push morning energy into evening screens.

## Ritual UX Principles

### The Daily Habit Loop
```
Morning  →  Brief + Panchang  →  Set intention (< 45 seconds)
Evening  →  Reflection        →  Close the loop (< 60 seconds)
```

### Remedies must feel quietly restorative — never compulsory.

Every remedy is a **behavioral ritual invitation**, not a prescription.
Language rules for remedies:
- ✅ "You might try spending 5 minutes in silence before speaking."
- ✅ "On Saturdays, a simple act of service tends to ground this energy."
- ❌ "You must perform this ritual to pacify Saturn."
- ❌ "Failure to observe this remedy may worsen the influence."

### Planetary archetypes over data grids.

Planets are behavioral archetypes, not celestial objects to be analyzed.
Each planet card must surface:
1. **Archetype** — emotional identity (e.g., "The Master Teacher of Structure")
2. **Behavioral tendency** — daily relevance
3. **Shadow tendency** — self-awareness prompt
4. **Grounding guidance** — constructive integration cue

Never show raw degree grids as primary content. Degrees are ambient context only.

## Correlation Chart Constraints

Weekly rhythm and correlation data must follow strict language protocol:

**Permitted:**
- "Periods of increased Saturn activity appear to correlate with reduced emotional steadiness."
- "Moon in fast-moving Nakshatras tends to coincide with higher recorded vitality."
- "These are observational patterns, not causal predictions."

**Forbidden:**
- "Saturn caused your stress this week."
- "88% match — high confidence prediction."
- "Your energy was correctly predicted X% of the time."

Correlations must always be:
- Observational
- Subtle in visual weight
- Probabilistic in language
- Presented softly — never as diagnostic verdicts

---

## Notification Ethics

The product may:
- Send one quiet morning email (6:00 AM) if the user has opted in
- Surface a gentle evening reflection prompt (passive, not push)

The product must never:
- Send multiple daily notifications
- Use urgency-framed push copy ("Don't miss today's reading!")
- Escalate notification frequency to drive engagement
- Badge-count unread insights

---

## Narrative Constraints

### What guidance text must feel like:
A calm, wise elder who speaks briefly and with restraint.
Not a wellness chatbot. Not an encyclopedia. Not a prediction machine.

### Sentence construction rules:
- Prefer short paragraphs (2–3 sentences maximum per section)
- Use softening adverbs: "tends to", "may", "appears to", "often"
- Avoid certainty: never "will", "always", "definitely", "must"
- Write as if observing, not prescribing

### What the product should feel like:
> A quiet morning note from someone who knows your chart deeply
> and cares about your long-term emotional health —
> not about impressing you with astrological complexity.

---

## What to Protect Aggressively

As the product matures, the greatest threat is **feature accumulation**.

Protect:
- **Emotional spaciousness** — never fill every screen
- **Calm interpretive restraint** — resist explaining everything
- **Ritual continuity** — daily habit loop above all else
- **Trust architecture** — anti-exploitation is the moat

Signs the product is drifting:
- Any screen with more than 3 primary actions
- Any planetary event framed as requiring immediate attention
- Any engagement metric optimized for sessions-per-day rather than days-per-month
- Any copy that implies the user needs the product to be spiritually safe

---

## The Final Test

Before shipping any feature, answer:

1. Does it deepen the daily ritual habit?
2. Does it increase emotional trust?
3. Does it feel restful to encounter?
4. Would a calm, spiritually grounded person find it respectful?
5. Does it protect the user's self-sufficiency?

If any answer is No — delay it.
