# DivyaDrishti — Product Principles

## The Governing Law

> **Every feature must strengthen emotional habit loops.**

Before shipping anything, answer these five questions:
1. Does it improve **retention**?
2. Does it build **trust**?
3. Does it drive **daily engagement**?
4. Does it create **emotional resonance**?
5. Is it **shareable**?

If the answer is No to most — delay it.

---

## What This Product Is

**DivyaDrishti is calm daily guidance software.**

Not:
- An astrology analysis platform
- An AI assistant
- A SaaS productivity tool

But: **a daily emotional-temporal practice.**

## Design Rules

### Restraint is the premium signal.
Premium products are defined by what they **refuse** to add.

### One primary insight per screen.
The Daily page must feel spacious, sacred, intentional, calming.
Not: widget-heavy, analytics-heavy, card-explosion.

### Morning Brief = under 45 seconds.
Fast emotional calibration. Compress quality over intelligence complexity.

### Never use numeric scores for emotional states.
❌ `Energy: 8/10`
✅ `High Momentum` / `Reflective` / `Grounded` / `Expansive`

### Panchang stays ambient, not encyclopedic.
✅ `Rohini — Nourishing & grounding`
❌ Four-paragraph Nakshatra essays

### Reflection framing is observational, not deterministic.
✅ `"Your reflections have recently aligned with periods of increased Saturn pressure."`
❌ `"DivyaDrishti predicted your energy correctly 92% of the time."`

### Today's guidance is stable for the day.
Generate once. Never mutate mid-day. Consistency = trust.

### Today's Theme is always present.
A single emotional phrase: `"Quiet Consolidation"` / `"Disciplined Expansion"`
This is memorable, shareable, identity-forming.

### Cosmic Cards must feel premium editorial.
Not: cheesy horoscope graphics, AI quote cards.
Think: calm spiritual magazine. Beautiful enough to share proudly.

---

## The Anti-Features

Things we will never add to the core daily loop:
- Gamification scores
- Push notifications for every micro-event
- Astrology trivia / educational content
- AI-generated essays in the daily view
- Competing information density on one screen

## Future Horizons (Vedic-Financial Consult 5.0+)

To maintain its moat as a symbolic-operational timing advisor rather than a chatbot, DivyaDrishti’s next evolution governs two core cognitive frontiers:

### 1. Temporal Memory Shaping
The system must transition from isolated *chart interpretation* to **longitudinal behavioral cycle interpretation**.
- **Observational Targets**: Track how the seeker reacts dynamically across transits. Do they over-expand during Jupiter transits? Do they collapse or capitulate during Saturn delays? Do they repeat entry-timing errors under Rahu pressure?
- **Cognitive Pivot**: Future interactions must build upon this recorded memory, shifting the advice from abstract cycle warnings to hyper-personalized behavioral pattern recognition (e.g., *"This transit activates the same 8th-house tension as August 2025—during which you over-committed capital to relieve anxiety. Practice the same containment now."*).

### 2. Predictive Emotional Drift (Nervous System Lag)
External reality changes faster than the human nervous system. The advisor must explicitly model and predict **lagging emotional adaptation**.
- **The Invariant**: Do not imply that opportunity returning equals immediate psychological ease. When external transit/dasha pressure reduces, explicitly predict the emotional lag: e.g., *"External liquidity improves this month, but your nervous system is likely to continue operating under high-alert vigilance for the next 30 days. Treat the linger of fear not as active danger, but as physiological recovery lag."*
- **Psychological Moat**: Validating this emotional mismatch builds ultimate trust, transforming the engine from a basic prediction system into a deeply realistic behavioral-timing stabilizer.
