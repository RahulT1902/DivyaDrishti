# DivyaDrishti Design Language & Principles

This document defines the core aesthetic, emotional, and structural guidelines for **DivyaDrishti**. It serves as the single source of truth for engineering premium, modern Vedic astrology user experiences.

---

## 1. Emotional Tone: Guided Calmnness

Vedic astrology can be intimidating, technical, or overly deterministic. The goal of DivyaDrishti is to provide **guided calmness** and **emotional grounding**.

*   **Astrology-Native Trust Surface**: We celebrate the traditional science of Vedic chart systems (e.g., North Indian Diamond Kundli) because they establish absolute astrological legitimacy.
*   **Explainable Wisdom**: We do not just surface raw planets and coordinate degrees. Every data point is coupled with human-centric, actionable intelligence.
*   **Spiritual Atmosphere**: The interface must feel like a sanctuary—soft, welcoming, and quiet.

---

## 2. Sacred Restraint: The Anti-Kitsch Rule

It is easy to over-decorate Indian spiritual applications with flashing neon lights, heavy golden gradients, and low-fidelity Sanskrit icons. **We practice extreme aesthetic restraint.**

| Element | Allowed (Premium Sanctuary) | Disallowed (SaaS/Kitsch) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Colors** | Warm ivory, parchment neutrals, muted cream, deep charcoal, soft saffron accents, earthy gold. | Pure black, bright primary orange, neon yellows, excessive multi-color gradients. |
| **Symbols** | Subtle astronomical glyphs (`♃`, `♄`, `☾`), minimal geometric mandalas, elegant gold suns (`✦`). | Huge zodiac clip-arts, complex animated temple wallpapers, large 3D god/goddess images. |
| **Animations** | Muted hover elevations, calm opacity fades, micro-interactive scale shifts. | Pulsing glows, blinking alerts, flashing buttons, neon border animations. |
| **Typography** | Editorial Serif headings (e.g., Playfair, Outfit, or standard serif) blended with premium Sans-serif body. | Hard geometric technical fonts, standard monospace for non-data elements, generic sans. |

---

## 3. Color Usage Limits

*   **Parchment Foundation**: The background is a comforting warm ivory (`#F8F5EF`). Card components sit on clean white pages (`#FFFFFF`) with thin, warm earth-gold dividers (`#F1E7D0`).
*   **Deep Charcoal-Brown**: All primary text uses a soft charcoal-brown (`#3F2D1D`) rather than harsh pure black.
*   **Restrained Saffron**: Saffron/amber is used *exclusively* for active highlights, such as selected tabs or primary planet badges. Saffron must never exceed **10% of the visible viewport**.

---

## 4. Spacing Philosophy: Spacing as Breath

Whitespace in DivyaDrishti is **breathing room**. Space allows the user to absorb highly personal astrological revelations without feeling overwhelmed or anxious.
*   **Density over Clutter**: We achieve high information density not by packing elements together, but by establishing elegant, multi-column modular layouts (e.g., placing Planetary summaries and "Why This Matters" summaries directly adjacent to the Kundli chart).
*   **Quiet Hierarchy**: We use thin gold dividers, elegant spacing, and uppercase subheadings instead of loud background blocks.

---

## 5. Human Astrological Vocabulary

To maintain emotional resonance, we replace dry system-level and technical terms with gentle, organic, human-centric descriptions.

*   Instead of **"Astrological Resilience Metrics"**, we use:
    *   *Emotional Balance*
    *   *Inner Stability*
    *   *Mental Clarity*
*   Instead of **"Strong Planets" vs. "Weak Planets"**, we use:
    *   *Supportive Influences* (Planetary alignments working in your favor)
    *   *Sensitive Areas* (Planetary alignments demanding conscious attention)
*   Instead of **"Volatility Score"**, we use:
    *   *Phase Intensity*
