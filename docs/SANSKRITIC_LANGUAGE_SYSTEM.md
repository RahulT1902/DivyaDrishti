# Sanskritic Language System

This system establishes the linguistic framework for DivyaDrishti. In spiritual and astrological spaces, language acts as a direct conveyor of sacred atmosphere. Our Hindi mode is not an English-to-Hindi translation layer; it is a **culturally native, spiritually grounded Vedic Sanskritic experience**.

---

## 1. Core Atmosphere: How Hindi Mode Should Feel

*   **Spiritually Grounded**: Warm, soft, and respectful, mirroring the oral tradition of an experienced Pundit sitting under a banyan tree, drawing charts on sand.
*   **Calm & Reassuring**: Imbalances are presented as *Misalignment (असंतुलन)* rather than *Punishment (दण्ड)* or *Fear (भय)*. 
*   **Contemplative & Reflective**: Avoid clinical or corporate self-help words (e.g. do not translate "productivity tracker" to "उत्पादकता ट्रैकर"; instead use "कर्म संरेखण" — alignment of action).

## 3. Climate & Reflective Forecasting

Vashistha Jyotish reads transit forces like "weather" or "energy currents." Avoid mechanical or fatalistic phrasing:

### A. Cosmic Weather States
*   *Supportive Cycle* -> **अनुकूल प्रवाह (Anukool Pravah)** — Supportive flow.
*   *Sensitive Cycle* -> **संवेदनशील चक्र (Sanvedansheel Chakra)** — High attention cycle.
*   *Stable Phase* -> **स्थिर एवं संतुलित अवधि** — Anchored phase.

### B. Prohibited Literal Translations (नकारात्मक अनुवाद)
*   **Avoid**: "High Volatility" -> "उच्च अस्थिरता" (feels chaotic/financial).
    *   **Use instead**: "सक्रिय ऊर्जा परिवर्तन (Active Energy Shifts)".
*   **Avoid**: "Celestial Pressure" -> "आकाशीय दबाव" (sounds mechanical or physics-like).
    *   **Use instead**: "दैवीय अनुशासन / सघन आत्म-मंथन का काल (Period of intense inner churning)".
*   **Avoid**: "Extreme Caution" -> "अत्यधिक चेतावनी" (triggers anxiety).
    *   **Use instead**: "सौम्य जागरूकता एवं संयम (Gentle awareness and restraint)".

## 4. Sentence Rhythm & Pacing Guidelines

Hindi prose in a spiritual software context should have a slow, breathing cadence. 

*   **Pacing**: Favour multi-clause, rhythm-based flow over short, punchy, corporate statements. Let thoughts unfold sequentially.
*   **Honorifics**: Address the user's path with reverence.
*   **Vocal Delivery Pattern**: Match the syntax of oral commentary.

### Example: Comparative Translations

*   **English**: *“Your Saturn transit requires tight budget discipline to prevent cash drain.”*
*   **Literal / Corporate Hindi (Avoid)**: *“आपका शनि पारगमन नकद निकासी को रोकने के लिए तंग बजट अनुशासन की मांग करता है।”* (Robotic, sounds like a credit card warning).
*   **Sanskritic Alignment Hindi (Target)**: *“द्वितीय भाव में शनि देव का यह गोचर आपकी वाणी एवं संचित धन में गंभीर अनुशासन की अपेक्षा रखता है। आवेश में आकर व्यय करने से बचें और स्थायित्व पर ध्यान केंद्रित करें।”* (Contemplative, soft, framing restriction as a positive practice of containment).
