# UX Philosophy: Modern Spiritual Astrology

## Core Positioning
**DivyaDrishti is a modern spiritual intelligence platform rooted in Vedic astrology.**
It is NOT a SaaS productivity tool, a behavioral operating system, or a chaotic traditional astro-portal.

## Emotional Target
The user must feel:
- **Calm**
- **Spiritually Grounded**
- **Emotionally Reassured**
- **Understood by the Cosmos**

## Visual Identity & Aesthetics
**"Apple-quality Indian Astrology"**
- **Colors**: Deep cosmic indigo, warm gold, subtle saffron, soft cosmic gradients.
- **Lighting**: Soft glows, sacred geometry, glassmorphism.
- **Avoid**: Pure black, neon green/purple (cyberpunk), abstract fintech charts, or chaotic 2005-era flashing elements.
- **Typography**: Clean, premium, highly legible, but warm.

## Terminology Contract
Never use abstract corporate/tech jargon. Always ground intelligence in astrology and spirituality.

| Avoid (SaaS / Abstract) | Use Instead (Spiritual / Familiar) |
|-------------------------|------------------------------------|
| Life OS | Vedic Intelligence Platform |
| Execution Mode | Recommended Approach |
| Momentum | Planetary Support / Current Strength |
| Life Rhythm | Planetary Timeline |
| Transition Alert | Upcoming Planetary Shift |
| Internal State | Emotional Balance |
| Strategic Focus | Focus Areas |
| Avoid Now | Areas of Caution |

## Core UI Anchors
The following elements must NEVER be hidden. They are the psychological anchors of trust:
1. **Kundli**: The ultimate anchor of identity.
2. **Dashas**: The major life chapters (Mahadasha, Antardasha).
3. **Transits**: Current planetary weather.
4. **Remedies**: Practical, calming, non-superstitious practices (mantras, fasting, meditation).
5. **Ask DivyaDrishti**: The warm, trustworthy AI conversational guide.

## The Architectural Rule
**The backend remains deeply intelligent, deterministic, and explainable.**
**The frontend remains symbolic, spiritual, and emotionally resonant.**
Do not let the UI expose the mechanical complexity of the intelligence engine. Let it surface the *meaning*.
